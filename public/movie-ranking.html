The Best Movies According to Idrisi

My Top 3 Favorite Movies

Neon Genesis Evangelion

A deep, psychological mecha anime that explores human nature, existentialism,
brand trauma, wrapped in an intense apocalyptic storyline with incredible animation and symbolism.

Ghost in the Shell

A cyberpunk masterpiece that questions identity, consciousness, and the
fusion of humanity with technology, featuring stunning visuals and a thought-provoking narrative.

Alita: Battle Angel

A gripping sci-fi action story about self-discovery, resilience, and
fighting against oppression, with a visually stunning world and an unforgettable protagonist.
